# PagePilot AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

PagePilot AI is a Chrome extension that provides AI-powered features including page summarization, writing assistance, and AI chat. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect

### Local Storage Only
- **API Keys**: Your OpenAI or Qwen API keys are stored locally in your browser's extension storage. We do not have access to your API keys.
- **Chat History**: Conversation history is stored locally in your browser.
- **Usage Data**: We track daily usage counts locally to enforce free tier limits.

### No Personal Information
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

## Third-Party Services

### AI API Providers
When you use AI features, your prompts and content are sent directly to:
- **OpenAI** (api.openai.com) - See [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **Alibaba Qwen** (dashscope.aliyuncs.com) - See [Alibaba Privacy Policy](https://www.alibabacloud.com/trust-center/privacy)

Your data is processed by these providers according to their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they handle your data.

## Data Usage

Your API key is used solely to authenticate requests to AI service providers. We do not:
- Collect or store your browsing history
- Track the websites you visit
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for any purpose other than providing AI features

## Data Control

You can control your data through the extension settings:
- **Clear API Key**: Remove your stored API key
- **Clear Chat History**: Reset conversation history
- **Clear All Data**: Remove all stored data

To clear data, open the extension popup, go to Settings, and click "Reset".

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
