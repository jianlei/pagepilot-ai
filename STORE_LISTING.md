## Description (Detailed)

### Overview
PagePilot AI brings the power of artificial intelligence directly to your browser. With just a click, access a powerful AI assistant that works alongside any webpage.

### Features

**📄 Smart Page Summarization**
- Instantly summarize any webpage
- Extract key points in seconds
- Perfect for research and quick reading

**✍️ Writing Assistant**
- Improve your writing with AI suggestions
- Shorten or expand text effortlessly
- Professional tone adjustments

**💬 AI Chat**
- Ask questions while browsing
- Get instant explanations
- Context-aware conversations

**🔒 Privacy-First Design**
- Your API keys stay on your device
- No data sent to our servers
- Direct connection to AI providers

**🌐 Multi-Provider Support**
- OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o Mini)
- Alibaba Qwen (Turbo, Plus, Max)
- Choose your preferred AI model

### How It Works
1. Add PagePilot AI to Chrome
2. Enter your API key in settings (supports OpenAI & Qwen)
3. Click the extension icon to open the sidebar
4. Start chatting, summarizing, or improving content

### Pricing
- Free tier: 10 AI requests per day
- Pro version: Unlimited access (coming soon)

---

**Note**: You need your own API key from OpenAI or Alibaba Qwen to use AI features. The extension does not include API credits.
